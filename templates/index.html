📧 Send Absentee Alerts
Sends an email to each absent student and a summary report to the admin. Configure SMTP settings via environment variables on Render.
Required Env Vars on Render
SMTP_USER — your Gmail address
SMTP_PASS — Gmail App Password
ALERT_TO — teacher/admin email
ALERT_FROM — sender display email
ℹ️ How Alerts Work
Step by step
1
Register students with email
Add student email in the Register section.
2
Set SMTP vars on Render
Go to Render dashboard → Environment → add the 4 vars.
3
Click Send Alerts
System checks who is absent today and emails each one + sends a summary to admin.
✓
Emails delivered
Students get individual alerts. Teacher gets full absentee report.
